Food Access Partnership

How can a dollar generate data?

Addressing food equity on Vashon Island with a unique, data-driven approach.

We reimagined how a dollar can generate data.

Food Access Partnership on Vashon Island distributes cash to the community to minimize costs of locally grown food, but the paper currency they create and distribute suffers from low redemption rates. Improving this system, our solution provides real time data for partnering nonprofits and farmers while preserving the familiar paper “Farm Buck.” Now, our stakeholders can track Farm Bucks through the system and encourage users to redeem them at farmer’s markets and farm stands. Placing data in the hands of organizers, we’re helping Vashon Islanders address food equity in their community.

How it works

Making Money

Organizers use our dashboard to create a PDF of unique bucks that is sent to the local print shop.

Spreading the Wealth

Caseworkers scan the Farm Bucks and hand them out to clients, spreading the currency throughout Vashon.

Growers get Paid

Farm Bucks are spent at the local farmers’ market. Farmers can scan the bucks to collect data and alert organizers that they need to be paid.

From Dollars to Data

Farm Bucks move through the system and generate data for our custom dashboard. Now, caseworkers and organizers can take action with this valuable realtime redemption data.

Our impact

Our team spent ten weeks conducting user research and adopting a value sensitive design approach to our solution. Understanding that our product will impact numerous diverse communities on Vashon Island, we chose a feasible solution with the highest impact.

Track VIGA Farm Bucks

Food Access Partnership is now able to have a comprehensive understanding of the flow of VIGA Farm Bucks. They will use this data to make better informed decisions related to the distribution of Bucks to partner organizations. Additionally, this data can be used in support of grant applications to recieve additional funding for their program.

Encourage Redemption

Caseworkers of the partnering non-profit organizations are now able to know how many of the bucks they hand out are redeemed back at the farmer's market or a farm stand. This allows caseworkers to be informed and encourage their clients to redeem the bucks if there is a low redemption percentage.

Empower Non-proft Organizations

The partnering non-profit organizations of Food Access Partnerhsip are the backbone of the system. Caseworkers at non-profits hand out the VIGA Farm Bucks to their clients and encourage redemption. Non-profits are now able to keep track of how many bucks are redeemed compared to how many they hand out. In near real time, they are able to stay up to date and informed.
